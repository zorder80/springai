Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analysis in a Large Language Model (LLM) refers to the model's ability to determine the emotional tone or opinion expressed in a text

It identifies whether the text expresses a positive, negative, or neutral sentiment. It recognizes specific emotions such as happiness, anger, sadness, or surprise. It identifies the sentiment expressed in relation to specific aspects or entities within the text (Aspect-based Sentiment Analysis - ABSA).
